# Reflection — Assignment 1: Communicating YOU

*Maximum 100 words. Answering all three questions below.*

---

**What was hardest to decide?**
Choosing one symbol for the split in me between the technical and the cultural, since I could not choose between the two.
Every version felt either too clean or too abstract. The seam only worked when I
stopped trying to resolve the tension and let it be visible.

**What did you remove?**
Colour, labels, and ornamentation. An earlier version had text nnotations inside the mark.
Removing them made the silence between the halves louder.

**What does this design say about you?**
The design shows my interest in computer and cultural networking.
